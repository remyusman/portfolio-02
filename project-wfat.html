We Forgot About This

A joint exhibition between myself, Eva Vidan, and Grace Colbert, "We Forgot About This" humorously explores how fast turnaround and a near-constant barrage of online content negatively affects design. Aiming to critique the stress and occasional creative block inherent in the design process (and enjoy an inside-joke amongst designers) we presented ‘unfinished’ work generated through a set of artificial and incidental production constraints.

Using the wood shop’s discarded scraps, painter’s tape, tiled posters, and misprints from a conveniently broken-down printer, we poked fun at the challenges of design and production when materials and time are scarce.
