He Scream At Own Ass: Memetics in the Digital Age

This 190-page book examines the history and impact of Internet memes on contemporary culture. Inspired by the 99% Invisible podcast episode “The Laff Box,” He Scream At Own Ass borrows its title from an opossum meme of the same name and is hand-bound using the Coptic stitch.

Scope of work: Print Design
