Black Cherry: A Discussion on Blaxploitation Film

A compilation of essays discussing the era and impact of Blaxploitation film in the '70s, Black Cherry is 176 pages of curated written and visual content. 6.5 x 9" in size, this project booklet takes inspiration from the decades' design and color schemes, and is packed with original behing-the-scenes photography of the featured films, and a compiled script at the end to capture the visceral language of the genre.
