Cobb County Public Library System

During the summer of 2017 I worked at the Cobb County Public Library System as a graphic design intern. The library system serves one of Georgia's fastest-growing counties, and is committed to providing a welcoming space and variety of resources for the public.

​

Their variety of print and digital resources continue to keep pace with an increasingly technological society, and I was asked to propose a redesigned brand identity that matches this forward-thinking attitude.

​

CCPL serves a diverse community of all ages and all backgrounds, which I sought to express in a colorful logo that references the community and it connections. A color scheme derived from the logo carries through the stationary and library cards, which may be customized according to their distinction (reader, teacher, donor, etc.)
