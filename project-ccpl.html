Cobb County Public Library System

Georgia’s Cobb County Public Library System serves one of the state’s fastest-growing counties and is committed to providing resources and a welcoming space for the public. In summer 2017 I was asked as their intern to propose a redesigned brand identity that captures the library’s warmth and serviceability.

CCPL serves a diverse community of all ages and all backgrounds, which I sought to express in a colorful logo that references the community and its connections. The palette derived from the logo carries through the stationary and other deliverables including bookmarks, monitor templates, and library cards.

Scope of work: Animation, Print, Identity Design
