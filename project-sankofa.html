Sankofa: Afrofuturism Catalogue

Through curated imagery and online articles, this 100-page book catalogs the historical and contemporary aesthetics of the Afrofuturism genre. The title itself is an homage to the 1993 Burkinabé film of the same name. With its limited palette, strict grid, and typography reminiscent of the genre-defining typeface Eurostile, the catalogue evokes contemporary science-fiction imagery.

Scope of work: Editorial Design
