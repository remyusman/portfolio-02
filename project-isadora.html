Isadora Concept Website

An influencer's desire to optimize her internet presence beyond social media platforms in a way that felt more personal than a page of nested links inspired this concept website. Isadora, a popular fashion photographer and an entrepreneur in the arts, wishes to share longer articles, news, and projects with her followers. The website includes space for all this, plus an online shop carrying items featured in sponsored posts, products from collaborations with brands, and an about page that also invites future collaborations.

Scope of work: Web Design, Animation
