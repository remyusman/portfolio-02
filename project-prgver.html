PRG / VER

PRG/VER is a mid-sized corporation supplying production technology and equipment for TV & film, theater, events, and more. First merged in 2017, the company commissioned me to animate the co-branded logo lockup in a way that illustrated the company’s formation and its brands’ connection. For PRG/VER's sales conference, Next Level, I also designed event branding, including logo and animation, badges, a wayfinding template, and a presentation template with an animated transition.

Scope of work: Animation, Logo Design, Event Identity
