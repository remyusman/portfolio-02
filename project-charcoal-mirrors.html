Charcoal Magazine, Issue 01: Mirrors

Charcoal is a print publication dedicated to celebrating student artists of color and their stories. Exploring the intersections of identity and art through reader submissions and original content, the magazine publishes bi-annually, each semester. The issues follow a theme for submissions and essays that cover important aspects of life.

The inaugural issue, Mirrors, implores readers to examine their identities and asks, "When you look in the mirror, what do you see?" As creative director I designed several spreads within the issue and created the promotional video, which I shot on-set of our cover shoot.
