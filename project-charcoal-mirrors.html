Charcoal Magazine, Issue 01: Mirrors

Charcoal Magazine is a print publication dedicated to celebrating student artists of color and their stories. Exploring the intersections of identity and art through reader submissions and original content, the magazine features themes covering important aspects of life and publishes bi-annually.

The inaugural issue, Mirrors, asks: When you look in the mirror, what do you see? As creative director I designed several spreads within the issue and created the promotional video that I shot on-set of our cover shoot, “Introspection.”

Scope of work: Art Direction, Photography, Print, Website Design
