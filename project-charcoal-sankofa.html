Charcoal Magazine, Issue 03: Sankofa

In Spring 2018 Charcoal Magazine released our third issue, Sankofa, which asked our readers to submit work that explores their roots and familial histories. Sankofa reminds us to look to the past in order to move forward, encouraging us to find the best parts of ourselves in our roots, history, and traditions and use them to better our future. Our cover story features an extended article about Filipinx artists Nikka Palapar, Rosie Reyes, and Casey Ramós, who collectively inspired this issue's golden color palette and voice.

As creative director I conducted Q+A-style interviews, conceptualized and art-directed stories, and designed article spreads.

Scope of work: Print, Website Design
