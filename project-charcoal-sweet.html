Charcoal Magazine, Issue 02: Sweet

Charcoal Magazine is a print publication dedicated to celebrating student artists of color and their stories. Released in Fall 2018, our second issue, Sweet, asked readers: What makes you happy? What makes life sweet? The prompt generated our highest number of artist submissions that explored themes of self-love, community, performance, and pride.

As creative director I conducted Q+A-style interviews, conceptualized and art-directed stories, and designed article spreads, as well as redesigned our website using Wix. Our issue launch featured student poetry, music, and discussion. The overall issue of the vibe embodies warmth, sensuality, and growth.

Scope of work: Print, Website Design

Photography also by Eva Vidan and Patricia Ho. For the complete list of contributors, please visit www.charcoalmag.co/issues-sweet.
