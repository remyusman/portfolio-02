Hi!

I’m Remy. Green is my favorite color and my two passions in life are design and following dogs on Instagram.

Originally from Atlanta, I reside in Boston part-time as a graphic design student at Boston University. Last summer I participated in Innovate@BU’s Summer Accelerator and worked as a freelance designer. I also designed and coded this site!

When in Boston I am a full-time dog watcher, a Posse Scholar, and founder & creative director of Charcoal Magazine: BU’s first and only art magazine celebrating student artists of color. When I’m not working I’m usually reading, traveling, or taking pictures of designer’s notebooks.
