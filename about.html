Hi!

I’m Remy. I’m a graphic designer who’s passionate about storytelling and drawn to projects with a purpose.

Based in Atlanta, I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in graphic design from Boston University, where I founded and led Charcoal Magazine: a student-run art publication celebrating student artists of color. I’m currently freelancing, and when I’m not working I’m usually reading, traveling, or following dog accounts on Instagram.

You can reach me at remyustudio@gmail.com.
