WarnerMedia TBX

WarnerMedia is a multinational media and entertainment company with headquarters in Atlanta, GA, where I recently interned under the Brand Experience team. Projects included supporting the wider team in creating production files and generating mockups, as well as designing environmental and internal marketing collateral, such as digital e-boards or social media templates, while ensuring that designs matched the new visual identity guidelines.

Scope of work: Digital + Environmental Design
